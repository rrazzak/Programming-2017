Welcome to Do You Wanna Be A Human!

Controls: 
Jump: up arrow
Double Jump: tap up arrow twice
Left: left arrow
Right: right arrow
Spacebar: Shoot
Melee Attack: Down Arrow
Escape: Pause menu

Players Start off with 10 lives.
Obstacles will cause the player to lose 1 life
Enemies will cause the player to lose a different amount of lives depending on the enemy.

Objective of the game is to collect all 4 heart pieces. There are heart pieces hidden throughout each level. Collect all four heart pieces and you win the game! :)
